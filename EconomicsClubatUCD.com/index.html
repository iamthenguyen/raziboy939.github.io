About Us

We are the official department club at UC Davis, designed to introduce students to economic research and related fields. We hope to build up interest and camaraderie among economic undergrads and those interested in economics.

We will acquaint you with the essentials that will aid your professional and personal life. We are here to help students succeed in their future endeavors in economic related professions.

We welcome students of all majors to become a part of the Economics Club. Come help us create the premier community at UC Davis.
